# Product Vision

## Mission

Help individuals understand and improve their financial life with the assistance of AI.

## Problem Statement

Most personal finance tools require too much manual effort and provide limited insights.

## Vision

Provide an AI-first personal finance platform that makes recording, understanding, and improving finances simple.

## MVP Goals

- Record expenses
- Record income
- Transfer money
- View balances
- View spending
- Ask AI about finances

## Principles

- User-first
- AI-assisted, not AI-dependent
- Simple by default
- Production quality
- Low operating cost
